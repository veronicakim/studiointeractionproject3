I imagine what it would be like to open my hand,
gently, so that a feather or a bird could land on it

(I know how cheesy that sounds, but I say it anyway).

I dial down the seven inputs of experience

(five senses, thoughts, and feelings)

and tune in to the constant current
of my witnessing consciousness.

I notice my heart when it closes,
then let the waves of feelings
flow through my heart, unstuck.
